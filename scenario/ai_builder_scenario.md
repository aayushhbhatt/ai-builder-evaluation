# Enterprise AI Builder Scenario

An enterprise operations team is reviewing short AI Builder work samples. Reviewers need a consistent way to inspect evidence about problem framing, builder execution, workflow fit, responsible AI boundaries, and communication of tradeoffs.

The review workbench should help reviewers organize evidence and notes without making candidate selection decisions. AI may extract evidence and suggest follow-up questions, deterministic code may verify quote presence, and humans remain responsible for all rubric judgments.
